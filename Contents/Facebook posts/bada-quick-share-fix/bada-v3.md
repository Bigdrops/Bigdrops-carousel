# Bada Quick Share Fix - Facebook Variation 3 (Huawei Users Saved)

Huawei owners: you know the struggle. No Quick Share, no easy way to send files to your Mac or PC. Bada fixes that.

It's open-source, no Google Play Services required. Download the APK from GitHub, install it, and you've got Quick Share on your Huawei phone.

It also works with NearDrop on macOS and Quick Share on Windows. So your Huawei can share files with Macs and Windows PCs without any third-party workarounds.

Tested on Xiaomi 17 Pro Max. Works without issues. The app shows up in your share sheet and functions like native Quick Share.

If you or someone you know uses a Huawei, send them this. Finally a real solution.
