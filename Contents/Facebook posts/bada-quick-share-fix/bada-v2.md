# Bada Quick Share Fix - Facebook Variation 2 (Tech Breakdown)

Quick Share doesn't work on Chinese phones. No Google Play Services means no native file sharing. Bada fixes that.

It's an open-source app from GitHub. No account, no Play Store, no restrictions. Install the APK and go.

The setup is straightforward: download from GitHub releases, install, grant permissions. On Xiaomi, enable unrestricted background usage so the system doesn't kill it. Takes maybe 30 seconds.

It bridges to NearDrop on macOS and Quick Share on Windows too. Your Huawei or Xiaomi can now share files with virtually any device.

No AirDrop support. But for everything else, Bada fills the gap.

Share this with someone who uses a Huawei or Xiaomi phone. They'll thank you.
