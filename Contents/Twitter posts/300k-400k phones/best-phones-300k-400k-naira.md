"Brand New" is a trap if you only have ₦400,000. Don't waste your money on a mid-range phone. Buy old powers.

We ranked 18 setups that humiliate 2026 mid-rangers in speed, gaming, and cameras. Zero retail bias. 🧵

---

#1 Xiaomi 14. SD 8 Gen 3. Leica. 4K selfie. 90W. Compact body. Runs cooler than everything here. iPhone 15 Pro has the same chip and costs 3x more. Best overall package. Argument closed.

![Xiaomi 14](mi14.jpg)

---

#2 Redmi Turbo 4 Pro. 6,000mAh battery, 90W. SD 8s Gen 3. Flat screen. Tank build. Buy this if you need a phone that outlasts everything on a single charge. Don't buy it for photography.

![Redmi Turbo 4 Pro](turbo4pro.jpg)

---

#3 Vivo X100. Dimensity 9300 all-big-core. Zeiss periscope zoom makes 5x look like you were standing next to the subject. Selfie is 1080p, noticeable. Curved, respect the edges.

![Vivo X100](vivox100.jpg)

---

#4 Honor Magic 5 Pro. 4K selfie. High-frequency PWM dimming means no eye strain after 3 hours scrolling. Most underrated phone in Nigeria right now and it's not close.

![Honor Magic 5 Pro](magic5pro.jpg)

---

#5 Xiaomi 13 Pro. Same 1-inch Sony IMX989 as the X90 Pro. SD 8 Gen 2, 120W. The selfie is 1080p32fps. For a phone with this camera reputation the front camera is a quiet betrayal.

![Xiaomi 13 Pro](mi13pro.jpg)

---

#6 OnePlus 11. SD 8 Gen 2, clean OxygenOS, no bloatware. Camera is solid, not spectacular. This phone's strength is daily usability. Fast, smooth, dependable. That reliability is rarer than people think.

![OnePlus 11](oneplus11.jpg)

---

#7 Samsung Galaxy S23. Most polished compact Android on this list. Chip optimized specifically for Samsung. Tight software, 4 years updates. The battery situation is an insult Samsung will never apologize for. Buy it anyway.

![Samsung Galaxy S23](s23.jpg)

---

#8 Vivo X90 Pro. 1-inch Sony IMX989 + V2 chip. Low light shots that look like studio lighting when you're just standing outside at night. Selfie is 1080p. Your photographer friend with a DSLR is not safe.

![Vivo X90 Pro](vivox90pro.jpg)

---

#9 Xiaomi 13. Good phone. No drama. SD 8 Gen 2 in a compact body. Leica rear, capable but no 1-inch sensor. Selfie is 1080p32fps. Xiaomi put Leica on the rear and forgot the front camera existed.

![Xiaomi 13](mi13.jpg)

---

#10 Google Pixel 8. Tensor G3 fixed the thermal embarrassment of the G2. 7 years of updates until 2031. Offline AI that works without internet. Computational photography works both cameras. Carry a powerbank.

![Google Pixel 8](pixel8.jpg)

---

#11 iQOO Neo 9. SD 8 Gen 2, 120W on a 5,160mAh battery. Back to full faster than most people finish breakfast. Camera is average. CN ROM. If you game hard and charge fast, this phone makes sense.

![iQOO Neo 9](iqooneo9.jpg)

---

#12 Redmi K70. Flagship killer. SD 8 Gen 2 at this price is the whole point. Gorilla Glass Victus. Ragdolled through atrocious tests and took zero damage. Camera? Average. Nobody buying a K70 for photos.

![Redmi K70](redmik70.jpg)

---

#13 Oppo Find X6. Genuinely strong camera experience. Hasselblad color science. The issue isn't that it's bad. The competition above it just offers stronger overall packages at similar pricing. Curved, CN ROM.

![Oppo Find X6](findx6.jpg)

---

#14 Redmi K60 Pro. Still a strong performance phone. Its biggest problem is being compared directly to the newer K70. K70 charges faster, is newer, at similar price. Only pick K60 Pro for that extra 500mAh.

![Redmi K60 Pro](redmik60pro.jpg)

---

#15 Xiaomi 12S Ultra. Age is the only thing working against this phone. 1-inch Sony IMX989, Leica. This camera still outperforms phones ranked above it. Buy it for one reason only: rear camera is your entire priority.

![Xiaomi 12S Ultra](mi12sultra.jpg)

---

#16 Xiaomi 14T. Good display, good cameras, good battery, one of the best selfies at 4K. Its issue: at this price buyers can find SD 8 Gen 2 and stronger. The 4K selfie is a consolation prize.

![Xiaomi 14T](mi14t.jpg)

---

#17 ROG 6D Ultimate. For sustained gaming nothing here competes. Thermal performance and frame stability are exceptional. 18GB RAM. As an everyday phone it asks for compromises. Incredible gaming device. Average all-round flagship.

![ROG 6D Ultimate](rog6dultimate.jpg)

---

#18 ROG Phone 6D. Still one of the most aggressive gaming phones ever built. 165Hz, air triggers, massive battery. The problem isn't gaming. It's everything outside gaming. If you're spending this money on a ROG, spend it on the Ultimate.

![ROG Phone 6D](rog6d.jpg)
